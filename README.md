# 🏠 Real Estate Market & Transaction Analysis

## 📌 Overview

Built an end-to-end SQL analytics project to analyze real estate transactions, pricing trends, housing demand, and investor activity. The project leverages MySQL to perform data exploration, cleaning, market analysis, and business intelligence reporting to support data-driven decision-making for investors, developers, and real estate stakeholders.

**Workflow:** Data Exploration → Data Cleaning → Market Analysis → Buyer & Investment Analysis → Business Insights & Recommendations

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Real estate markets are influenced by changing property prices, buyer preferences, housing supply, and investor activity. Without structured analysis, it becomes difficult for stakeholders to:

* Identify high-growth markets.
* Understand affordability challenges.
* Evaluate investment opportunities.
* Monitor market competitiveness.
* Make informed pricing and development decisions.

This project aims to transform raw real estate data into actionable business insights using SQL.

## 🔍 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Exploration & Quality Assessment

* Evaluated record completeness across multiple tables.
* Measured missing value percentages in key columns.
* Identified duplicate records.
* Detected extreme pricing values and unrealistic property sizes.

### 2. Data Cleaning & Integrity Management

* Standardized missing property types.
* Created reusable templates for median and mean imputation.
* Implemented duplicate removal strategies.
* Corrected extreme pricing values using the 99th percentile cap.

### 3. Market Trends & Pricing Analysis

* Analyzed average property prices by city and year.
* Calculated year-over-year price changes.
* Identified cities with the highest and lowest property values.
* Compared pricing across property types.
* Evaluated the impact of interest rates on property prices.

### 4. Buyer Behavior & Investment Patterns

* Identified cities with the strongest housing demand.
* Compared home prices and rental prices.
* Analyzed property size distributions by property type.

### 5. Housing Supply & Market Competitiveness

* Tracked new property developments over time.
* Identified cities with the highest construction activity.
* Studied the relationship between housing supply and prices.
* Measured changes in investor activity across cities.

### 6. Buyer Segmentation & Affordability Analysis

* Tracked investor activity trends over time.
* Calculated price-to-income ratios.
* Classified affordability stress across income groups.
* Ranked cities based on investor activity.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Property prices vary significantly across cities and years.
* Interest rate changes influence housing affordability and pricing trends.
* Some cities demonstrate consistently high housing demand and investor activity.
* New construction activity differs considerably across regions.
* Certain income groups experience severe affordability challenges.
* Investor activity is concentrated in a small number of high-growth cities.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Prioritize investments in cities with strong housing demand and stable price growth.
* Monitor affordability metrics before launching new developments.
* Use investor activity trends to identify emerging markets.
* Increase housing supply in high-demand regions to improve affordability.
* Develop city-specific pricing strategies based on local market conditions.
* Continuously monitor interest rate changes and their impact on property prices.
